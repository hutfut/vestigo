## Mission

Pulley's mission is to make it easier for anyone to start a company. They believe that more startups should exist, that founder-led companies are more successful long-term, and that equity — one of the biggest levers for economic mobility — shouldn't be a barrier to building.

## Products

### 1. Cap Table Management

The core product — a full equity management platform replacing spreadsheets and legacy tools like Carta.

**Equity Cap Tables:**
- Real-time ownership tracking across all security types (shares, options, RSAs, SAFEs, warrants)
- Employee equity plan creation (ESOPs, stock options)
- Interactive offer letters with equity visualization
- Investor and employee portals for self-service equity viewing
- E-signing for SAFE, Option, and RSA agreements (both templated and custom)
- Share certificates
- HRIS and payroll integrations
- Board approval workflows
- Vesting schedule management with acceleration support

**Token Cap Tables:**
- Token compensation plans and agreement signing
- Token vesting and lockup tracking
- Integration with crypto exchange platforms for automated distribution
- Works with any blockchain
- 83(b) election tracking

### 2. Compliance & Reporting

- **409A Valuations** — In-house team delivers defensible valuations in 3 days or less
- **ASC 718 Reporting** — Stock-based compensation reporting for audits
- **GAAP and IFRS** reporting standards support
- **Form 3921** generation
- **Rule 701** compliance tracking
- Direct integrations with major accounting systems
- Constant cap table monitoring for legal compliance

### 3. Fundraising Tools

- **Fundraise Modeling** — Scenario modeling for dilution, SAFE conversion, and exit outcomes
- **SAFE Issuance** — Send and sign SAFEs directly from the platform
- Real-time dilution and ownership visualization across funding scenarios
- Pro forma modeling for M&A, fundraising, and liquidity events
- **Data Room** functionality for investor diligence

### 4. Capital Transactions & Liquidity

- **Nasdaq Private Market Integration** — First cap table platform with NPM's Cap Table Connect product (launched October 2024)
- Tender offer management with automatic cap table data sync via API
- Secondary liquidity program design for employees and investors
- Post-trade settlement with programmatic book record synchronization
- Transfer restriction communication to all transacting parties

### 5. Employee Experience

- Employee equity portals showing vesting, value, and exercise options
- Interactive offer letters communicating equity value to candidates
- Communications Hub for stakeholder messaging
- Exercise workflow management

### 6. Crypto / Web3

- Token cap tables and distribution management
- Token valuations (starting at $10,000, 2–3 day turnaround)
- Non-custodial token distribution with admin approval
- Tax calculation and automatic withholding
- Payroll provider sync for token compensation
- Custodian and multi-sig integrations

## Market Position & Competition

**Primary Competitor:** Carta (~80% market share in cap table management)
**Pulley's Market Share:** ~15% and growing steadily

Pulley positions itself as "Carta without the complexity" — cleaner UI, faster onboarding, transparent pricing, and founder-first focus. Hundreds of companies have switched from Carta to Pulley.

**Other Competitors:**
- Shareworks (Morgan Stanley) — enterprise-focused
- Ledgy — European, employee-equity focused
- AngelList Stack — fundraising ecosystem integration
- Eqvista — comprehensive with unlimited 409A valuations
- Cake — startup-focused alternative

**Key Differentiators:**
- Founder-first philosophy (doesn't serve VCs or law firms as primary customers)
- No data monetization ("your data is your data")
- Transparent, predictable pricing
- Human expert support at every stage
- Organic growth only (no paid acquisition as of Series B)

## Company Culture

Pulley describes itself as a high-performing, remote-first team. Core values include:

- **Principles over playbooks** — Understand the "why" behind strategies
- **Momentum over speed** — Balance short-term wins with long-term success
- **Good taste over data** — Trust instincts for bold bets
- **Ideas over egos** — Best idea wins regardless of source
- **Gladiator over spectator** — Execute, don't just critique
